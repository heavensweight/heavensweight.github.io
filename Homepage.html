Born Into Islam. This Is What It Did to Me.

This is where I share my story — a story shaped by faith, fear, and the heavy expectations I was born into. My childhood wasn’t normal; it was filled with pressure and control that left deep scars.

I don’t expect anyone to have lived exactly what I did. Maybe no one ever has. But if you’ve ever struggled with beliefs that didn’t fit you, or felt trapped by family or tradition, you might understand some of this.

Here, I speak honestly about what religion took from me, and what I’m trying to reclaim: my freedom to think, to question, and to simply be myself. Thank you for being here.
